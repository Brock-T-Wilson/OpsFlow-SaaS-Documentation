# Supervisor Permissions

Supervisors manage operational workflows, approvals, scheduling,
and incident response activities.

## Supervisor Access

Supervisors can:

- approve work orders
- assign employee schedules
- escalate incidents
- review department analytics
- manage workflow approvals

## Operational Responsibilities

Supervisors are responsible for:

- staffing coverage
- operational efficiency
- escalation handling
- incident response coordination
