# Notification Delays

Notification delivery delays may affect workflow approvals,
incident escalations, and operational alerts.

## Common Causes

Notification delays may occur because of:

- email provider filtering
- delayed SMS delivery
- integration synchronization failures
- disabled notification preferences

## Troubleshooting Steps

1. Verify notification preferences are enabled.
2. Review integration status.
3. Confirm email delivery settings.
4. Retry notification delivery.
