# Reporting Questions

## Which export formats are supported?

OpsFlow supports:

- CSV
- PDF
- XLSX

---

## Why did my report export fail?

Large exports may timeout during peak operational periods.

Reduce the report date range and retry the export.

---

## Can dashboards be customized?

Dashboard customization features are planned for a future release.
