# Account Questions

## How do I reset my password?

1. Open the OpsFlow login page.
2. Select **Forgot Password**.
3. Follow the password reset instructions sent by email.

---

## Why is my account locked?

Accounts may become temporarily locked after multiple failed login attempts.

Contact your administrator if access cannot be restored automatically.

---

## How do I update my MFA device?

1. Open **Settings > Security**.
2. Disable the current MFA method.
3. Configure a new authenticator device.
